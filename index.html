BirdDex! is your structured bird database concept that blends real-world species info with game-like elements. Each bird entry includes scientific details (like weight, wingspan, and classification) alongside stylized features such as abilities and stats (e.g., speed, agility, song). It works like a mix between a field guide and a collectible index, making it both informative and interactive—almost like a Pokédex, but specifically for birds.
